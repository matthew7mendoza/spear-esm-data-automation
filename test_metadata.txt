Once upon a time, there was a ferocious rabbit
Such rabbit was as evil as could be,
So evil, in fact, that he had no glee.

Given this, the evil rabbit had to run, 
from the man happy like the shining sun, 
the evil rabbit didn't like the light, 
the evil rabbit would rather fight.

The rabbit is evil because of his troubled upbringing, 
He is angry towards the world.

The "other animals" look at the rabbit.
